Mailing lists have been divided into three groups:

  • F: Mainly foundation or chapter oriented
  • P: Mainly project oriented
  • T: Mainly technically oriented

Of course separation between lists is not absolute, e.g. sometimes technical matters are discussed on project lists.

On page Powerposters for each person three colours before his/her name indicate how many of the posts were in that category.
It can be used to match likewise inclined people in the list (of course don't take this too seriously).
