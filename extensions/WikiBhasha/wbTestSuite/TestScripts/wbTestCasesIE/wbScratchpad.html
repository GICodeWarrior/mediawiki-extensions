[Tags] BVT
  Log Summary :- Selecting huge amount of text in the Target article tab and copying it into the "Scratch pad" window    
  Log Description :- Verifying when user selects huge amount of text from Target article tab and copies it into the Scratch Pad window    
  Open Wikipedia ${wbLang} ${SourceSearchTextExist}  
  Execute_script ${BookMarklet}    
  Wait Until page contains element ${HistoryContainerID} ${DELAY}  
  Wait for Translation

  Click Element ${ScratchPadBtnID} Dont Wait

  Log ${Confirmation}    
  Test Pass
