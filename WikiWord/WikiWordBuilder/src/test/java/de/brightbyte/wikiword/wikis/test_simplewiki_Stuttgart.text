'''Stuttgart''' is a city in [[Germany]]. It is the capital of the [[States of Germany|state]]of [[Baden-Württemberg]] and of the [[Administrative District of Stuttgart]] (''Regierungsbezirk''). Stuttgart is on the river [[Neckar]] and has 590,000 inhabitants.

Stuttgart is currently the sixth largest German [[city]] and Stuttgart Region is the nation's fourth largest [[region]]. The city is divided into 23 city [[district]]s.

Stuttgart is also the home of two [[car]] manufacturers: [[Mercedes Benz]] and [[Porsche]].
Porsche is located at Zuffenhausen (North) and Mercedes at Untertürkheim (South-west).

==History==
*2006 Stuttgart was a playground of the [[World Cup 2006]].
