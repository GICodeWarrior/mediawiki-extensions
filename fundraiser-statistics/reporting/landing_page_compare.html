Custom Banner / LP Test:


To get the best results from this test use it across full hours. That is your start and end timestamps should be right on the hour (ie. ending in '0000'). The statistical test being used is the Wald test which assumes a normal distribution of the data. Due to the known variance over several hours each hour is tested indendently.

This test compates one metric across two landing pages running concurrently (which usually also entails running over the same campaign). So to test the performance of the two pages over some metric enter the times, camapaign(s), landing page names, and finally select the metric from the radio button to test.
