What is Xerver?

Xerver is a free web server and FTP server.
You can use Xerver for several purposes:

  • Run and test your CGI-scripts (Perl, PHP etc.) on your computer with a CGI-interface.
  • Allow your friends to download files from your hard drive.
  • Publish your homepage on internet with your own computer.
